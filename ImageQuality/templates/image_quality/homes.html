Food Image Attractiveness

Welcome

Welcome to our study! The aim of this study is to gain insights into how individuals make food choices based on the attractiveness of image associated with the food. This study is part of the PhD research of Ayoub El Majjodi at the University of Bergen, Norway, under supervision of Prof. Dr. Christoph Trattner and Dr. Alain Starke.
Please carefully read the procedure followed in this study to give your informed consent for voluntary participation*.

Procedure
Initially, you will be inquired about your personal cooking knowledge. Subsequently, you will be presented with a list of recipe pictures, from four different food categories: dessert, salads, pasta and snacks. Your task is to provide a ratings as to what extent you find the food image attractive and provide justification for your ratings, in total you will rate 12 recipes. Finally you answer 4 question regarding you general ratings and judgment.

Duration

The study takes around 8-12 minutes to complete. By clicking the 'Start' button, you agree to have read and understood this consent form.
